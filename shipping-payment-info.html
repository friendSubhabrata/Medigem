Shipping & Delivery Policy (Products)
For International buyers, orders are shipped and delivered through registered international courier companies and/or International speed post only.
For domestic buyers, orders are shipped through registered domestic courier companies and /or speed post only.
Orders are shipped within 7 working days for all the items in stock & within 4-6 weeks for the items which are out of stock at the time of order or as per the delivery date agreed at the time of order confirmation and delivering of the shipment subject to Courier Company / post office norms.
MEDIGEM is not liable for any delay in delivery by the courier company / postal authorities and only guarantees to hand over the consignment to the courier company or postal authorities within the above mentioned time period from the date of the order with 100% payment or as per the delivery date agreed at the time of order confirmation.
Please note that actual size or dimensions of the ordered product may be different as shown in the website. Packing, Design & Color may vary as may be available in our stock at the time of order. Delivery of all orders, in India, will be to the address specified at the time of order or registered address of the buyer as per the credit/debit card only at all times.
In case of delivery outside India, we are liable to dispatch the ordered goods maximum upto the nearest AIRPORT to the address specified at the time of order or registered address of the buyer as per the credit/debit card only at all times.
The customer, himself/herself has to arrange the pickup of their returned goods (if any, due to their respective country restrictions or other reason) , at their cost and also has to pay for any consequent expenses or losses, from our company address within 10 days of such returns. MEDIGEM is in no way responsible for any damage to the order while in transit to the buyer. Domestic buyers will need to provide any transit forms which may be required in their respective states.
